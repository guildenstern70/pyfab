Fairy tales

Fairy Tales teach moral lessons and promote reading, education and literacy to children. Presenting children an ebook about themselves will grab their attention and encourage them to read more often. Creating a personalized fairy tale is fast and easy. You can choose within a variety of fairy tales templates, provide the child's personalized data such as the name, and have the tale generated in PDF and ePub formats, readable on your mobile devices (Apple, Sony, Android, Kindle...)
